**Preview text:** Our single batches are no longer for open sale. Here's how to get one.

---

**[Chulele wordmark]**

**Held in Reserve**

There's something we want to tell you plainly.

Our single-batch mezcals — Día de los Muertos and Guelaguetza — are no longer listed for open sale. We still have bottles. We're choosing to hold them in reserve.

Each is one batch, tied to one harvest in Oaxaca. When a batch is finished, there is no more — not next month, not next year. Bottles that finite shouldn't be sold to the fastest cart. They should find the right hands.

So from now on, our reserve releases happen quietly, by allocation. No checkout to race. Just a list, and a note from us when a bottle is meant for you — or when the next harvest comes down from the hills.

If you'd like to be on it, leave your name.

With respect from Santiago Matatlán,
**The Chulele family**
