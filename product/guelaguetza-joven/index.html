The Batch

A mezcal
of offering

Guelaguetza is a Zapotec word for reciprocal giving — what you offer the community returns to you. Each July, Oaxaca turns it into one of Mexico's great festivals of music, dance, and offering.

We made this mezcal in that spirit — as an offering rather than a product. One batch of espadín from Santiago Matatlán: roasted in earth, fermented on wild yeast, twice-distilled in copper.

It pours as Espadín Joven at 50.4% ABV, 100% agave — a mezcal built for sharing. The table, the toast, the gathering.
