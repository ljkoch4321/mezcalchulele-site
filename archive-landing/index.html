DISCOVER OUR CHULELE® SINGLE BATCH MEZCALS

These single batch releases of Chulele® artisanal mezcals are our way to capture and share the magic and beauty that is Oaxaca. A land that carries traditions from the Zapotec and Mixtec peoples. Peoples who have made this land of mountains, Sierra and coast their home for hundreds of generations. 

Oaxaca is land of many pueblos and over thirty different spoken languages. A great culture survives here that many in the world have never heard of. Including our founder, who had no idea that such a rich world existed in the south of Mexico. People deeply connected to the land, using the natural resources for survival and to create great art, textiles and cuisine. Using their learned understanding of color, smell and flavor in their purist forms, passed down through the generations. Unaltered by modern materials and machines, the Oaxacan people continue to use these skills to create.

Artisanal mezcal is art, tradition and a journey. Each bottle of Chulele® artisanal mezcal is a unique story from the moment you open it. To uncork a bottle is to release all that was captured in the creation of this holisitc liquid. Experience one of our mezcals by letting it invade your nostrils and envelope your tongue as you explore the complexity of each single batch. All tell a different story and transport you to the moment, connecting you to the people where they were created.
